Summary

Hello! I am a student at the University of Washington pursuing a Bachelor's of Science degree in Informatics as well as a Bachelor's of Arts degree in Biochemistry. I thrive on combining information and design to produce seamless and visually aesthetic information systems.

I have a passionate interest for UI/UX Design and big data. I am also very good at performing and organizing multiple tasks at once, as well as communicating and working efficiently in an agile, fast paced team environment.
